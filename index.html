About TechWithRavin

Empowering Everyone to Fix Their Own Tech Problems — One Guide at a Time.

👋 Hey, I'm Ravin

Welcome to TechWithRavin, your trusted destination for easy, practical, and free tech help. I'm currently a Diploma in Information Technology student (Semester 4) with a strong passion for helping people solve their day-to-day technology issues — no complicated jargon, no confusing processes.

What started as fixing a few laptops and phones for friends and family turned into a mission: making tech support easy, accessible, and understandable for everyone — students, working adults, seniors, even small business owners.

🚀 What Is TechWithRavin?

TechWithRavin is more than just a website — it's a growing knowledge base and support center for common (and uncommon) tech problems. From fixing slow laptops, formatting USBs, or resetting Wi-Fi routers, to understanding terms like SSD, DNS, or IP — I break things down into step-by-step guides, video tutorials, and troubleshooting tips that anyone can follow.

🎯 My Vision & Mission

My Vision:

To become a go-to platform in Malaysia and beyond where anyone, regardless of tech experience, can find help, learn something new, and become more confident with technology.

My Mission:

  • To help 10,000+ people solve tech problems using free online content
  • To promote digital literacy for all age groups
  • To make self-repair and digital confidence possible without needing paid IT support

Common questions about TechWithRavin and tech support.

I help with a wide range of tech problems including:

  • Computer/laptop issues (slow performance, not turning on, hardware problems)
  • Mobile/tablet problems (battery drain, app crashes, storage issues)
  • Internet and networking (Wi-Fi problems, router setup, connection issues)
  • Software help (Microsoft Office, PDF, browser problems)
  • Security and privacy (passwords, malware removal, scam prevention)

If you're not sure if I can help with your specific problem, just ask!

You can send me a message anytime through the contact form, email, or social media. I typically respond within 24 hours, often much sooner. My usual availability is:

  • Weekdays: 9:00 AM - 10:00 PM (GMT+8)
  • Weekends: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM (GMT+8)

For urgent issues outside these hours, you can try WhatsApp or Telegram, but response time may vary.

  • Share my website with others who might need help

Yes! For problems that can't be solved through guides alone, I offer remote support using:

  • TeamViewer - For full remote control of your computer
  • AnyDesk - Alternative remote desktop software
  • Zoom/Google Meet - For video calls and screen sharing

Remote sessions are typically limited to 30-60 minutes for free support. For longer or more complex issues, we can discuss options based on your situation.

Important: I will never ask for payment upfront or request remote access without your explicit permission. Beware of scammers pretending to be tech support.

I'm glad you're interested in learning more! Here are some ways to build your tech skills:

  1. Start with my tutorials - I explain concepts in simple terms
  2. Practice on old devices - Try fixing or upgrading an old phone/computer
  3. Free online courses - Check out platforms like:
    • Codecademy (for coding)
    • Google Digital Garage (for basics)
    • Microsoft Learn (for Windows/Office)
  4. YouTube channels - Many great tech educators like:
    • Linus Tech Tips (for hardware)
    • Techquickie (for explanations)
    • ThioJoe (for tutorials)

Remember, everyone starts as a beginner. Don't be afraid to ask questions!

I can guide you through many common hardware issues like:

  • Cleaning dust from laptops/computers
  • Replacing RAM or storage drives
  • Troubleshooting display or keyboard issues
  • Basic phone repairs (battery replacement, screen issues)

However, for complex hardware problems or if you're not comfortable opening devices, I recommend:

  1. Checking if the device is under warranty first
  2. Consulting a professional repair service for:
    • Liquid damage repairs
    • Motherboard-level issues
    • Data recovery from damaged drives

I can help you understand what might be wrong and whether it's something you can fix yourself or need professional help with.
